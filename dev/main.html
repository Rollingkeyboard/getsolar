Photovoltaic system (PV) advisory web app calculator, meant to assist novice, residential owners with sizing a solar system to meet their annual energy loads. The website's goal is to increase the number of knowledgeable citizens who make well-informed decisions when purchasing solar products by estimating the energy production and cost of grid-tied photovoltaic systems at the residential scale.

- A photovoltaic system (PV) size estimate
